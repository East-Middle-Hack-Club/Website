East Middle Hack Club

East Middle School Hack Club

Welcome to the Official website of the East Middle Hack Club.

Hack Club is an after-school program that was started in September 2019. Members meet
on Wednesdays and Thursdays after school in room 428.

Our Goal For The Future

Our Goal is to promote computer science and computer engineering in the minds of middle school
students. Our plan is to allow them to explore these subjects and get an idea if they want to continue
this in high school and take specialty classes or if they want to take another path. If they like the
subject we allow them the time and people to work with to further their skills and help our community.

What?

Hack Club is a club that is sponsored by Hack Club and hosted at East Middle.
It is a club focused around coding and computer hardware.

Where?

Hack Club EMS is hosted by our teacher sponsor in his classroom.
It is room 428 at East Middle School, Sioux City, IA.

When?

Our members meet every Wednesday and Thursday after school from 2:45p to 4:00p.

Why

The Reason we created hack club at our school was so that young minds could learn to
code what they want to or learn how computers work. It is like an advanced Computer Technology class.

How?

The way the Hack Club works is that it is lead by the Student Leaders but is
still sponsored by a teacher. The job of the teacher is too be there for assitance
beyond what the studen leaders can provide.
